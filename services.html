OUR SERVICES

Expert Civil & Water Engineering Solutions

Amarre Engineering provides comprehensive civil and water engineering solutions for infrastructure projects. Our services span from preliminary design through construction oversight and technical consultation.

  • Hydraulic Structure Design

    Expert design and engineering of locks, dikes, and water management structures. Utilizing advanced modeling and proven methodologies for optimal safety and functionality.

  • Maritime Infrastructure

    Specialized design of mooring systems, floating structures, steel bridges, and maritime facilities. Complete structural analysis and engineering consultation.

  • Engineering Consulting

    Technical expertise in project feasibility studies, design reviews, construction methodology planning, and quality assurance throughout project lifecycle.

  • Safety & Risk Assessment

    Comprehensive evaluation of water safety systems, flood risk modeling, and infrastructure vulnerability assessment using probabilistic analysis and WBI standards.

  • Construction Oversight

    Professional supervision and quality control during construction phases. Detailed inspection and compliance verification with engineering specifications.

  • Detachment Services

    Experienced engineers available for project-based roles and consulting engagements with engineering firms and contractors.
